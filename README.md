## How it works

- Uses your webcam + [MediaPipe](https://google.github.io/mediapipe/) to detect your hand.
- Recognizes:
  - ‚úä Fist ‚Üí Rock
  - ‚úã Open palm ‚Üí Paper
  - ‚úåÔ∏è Two fingers ‚Üí Scissors
- Bot picks randomly.
- Text-to-speech announces each round‚Äôs result.
- First to 5 points wins!
